Privacy Policy

One Leaf — the app & justoneleaf.com · Last updated 26 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the One Leaf app and the website justoneleaf.com (together, “the app”), published by Future Proofing Lifestyle, handle your information. In short: the app is a simple book reader, and it collects no personal information about you at all.

The short version: No sign-up. No accounts. No ads. No tracking. No data leaves your device. The whole book is built into the app, so it works fully offline.

Information we collect

None. The app does not collect, request, or transmit any personal information. There is no account, no login, no sign-up, and no registration. We do not ask for your name, email, phone number, location, or contacts.

Information stored on your device

For your convenience, the app may save a few things locally on your own device only — such as your reading position, any notes you make, and your reading or narration settings. This information stays on your device, is never sent to us or anyone else, and is removed if you uninstall the app or clear its data.

No advertising or analytics

The app contains no advertisements, no analytics, and no third-party tracking tools. We do not build a profile of you and we do not share or sell any information, because we do not collect any.

Reading aloud (text-to-speech)

When you use the “Listen” feature, the narration is produced by your own device's built-in voice engine. Nothing you read or hear is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

Internet & offline use

The complete book is bundled inside the app, so you can read and listen with no internet connection. The app does not require a network connection to function.

Children

The app is suitable for readers of all ages. Because it collects no personal information from anyone, it collects none from children either.

Changes to this policy

If this app ever changes in a way that affects your privacy, we will update this page and revise the date above. Continued use of the app after an update means you accept the revised policy.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at omjai.vt@gmail.com.
